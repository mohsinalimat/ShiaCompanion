Account Deletion for Shia Companion

Welcome to the account deletion page for Shia Companion. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Shia Companion app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings page.
  3. Find the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Click on the "Delete Account" button to initiate the account deletion process.
  5. You will be asked to confirm your decision to delete your account and associated data.
  6. Once confirmed, your account and all related data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Please note the following:

  • We will delete all personal information associated with your account, such as your name, email, and any other provided details.
  • Your saved favorites and any other user-specific data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • We may retain some non-personal, anonymous data for analytical purposes, but this data will not be linked to your account or personally identifiable information.

If you have any concerns or questions about the account deletion process, please contact our support team at support@shiacompanion.com.

Thank you for using Shia Companion.
